Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trezor Bridge and why do I need it?

Trezor Bridge is a software that allows your Trezor hardware wallet to securely communicate with your web browser. It is essential for managing your cryptocurrencies through web interfaces like Trezor Suite or third-party apps. Unlike browser extensions, Trezor Bridge operates as a background service, providing a stable and secure connection without relying on specific browser versions. This ensures your private keys remain protected while interacting with online platforms.

How do I install and set up Trezor Bridge?

To install Trezor Bridge, visit the official Trezor website and download the latest version compatible with your operating system (Windows, macOS, or Linux). After downloading, follow the installation instructions. Once installed, Trezor Bridge will automatically run in the background whenever your computer is on. When you connect your Trezor device and open a supported web interface, Trezor Bridge will establish a secure connection, allowing you to manage your cryptocurrencies.

What should I do if Trezor Bridge is not detecting my Trezor device?

If Trezor Bridge is not detecting your Trezor device, first ensure that the software is running. You can check this by looking for the Trezor Bridge process in your system's task manager or activity monitor. If it’s not running, restart the service or reinstall Trezor Bridge from the official Trezor website. Additionally, try reconnecting your device, using a different USB port, or restarting your computer. If the problem persists, ensure your web browser is up to date and compatible with the Trezor Suite.
